Profile

Adam Dodd

Principal,

Adam initially trained as a Barrister and was called to the Bar in 2012, where he specialised in Criminal and Civil Litigation. Whilst at the Bar, he had a varied caseload and represented clients at every jurisdictional level (i.e. District Court, Circuit Court, High Court/Central Criminal Court, Special Criminal Court, Court of Appeal and Supreme Court). He was subsequently also called to the Bar of England and Wales. Adam later transferred to the Roll of Solicitors and practiced at a well-known firm, specialising in Personal Injury Litigation and Criminal Defence, before establishing Dodd Solicitors.

He is very experienced in courtroom advocacy and has a track record of obtaining exceptional results for his clients. He continues to regularly appear before the courts, advocating on behalf of his clients, as well as attending Garda Stations to advise clients during detention and interview. Adam has become well known for his sincerity and genuine care for the wellbeing of his clients. He is recognised as a legal practitioner that is always willing to go the extra mile for his clients.
