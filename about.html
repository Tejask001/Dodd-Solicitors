About Us

Dodd Solicitors was established with the goal of providing professional and specialised legal advice and services to members of the public. The firm’s focus is client-centred, rather than profit-centred. Legal proceedings, whether they be self-instituted or responding to a Garda or civil summons, can be daunting and overwhelming. We are here to simplify matters and help our clients successfully navigate the entire process. In line with the times that we now live in and the most recent, cutting edge developments in legal practice management; we operate a Virtual Office-model. This provides our clients nationwide with the best of traditional legal practice combined with all the convenience of modern technology- thereby disrupting our client’s lives as little as possible. Whether a client prefers in-person legal advice or a remote consultation, we have you covered.

Dodd Solicitors fearlessly advocate on behalf of our clients; ensuring that we consistently achieve the best results possible. Our meticulous attention to detail ensures client satisfaction and very high referral rates.
